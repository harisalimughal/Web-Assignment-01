AI-Powered Smart City Management System

Revolutionizing Urban Living Through Intelligent Technology

Project Scope

This Final Year Project aims to develop an innovative AI-powered system for efficient smart city management, focusing on enhancing urban living through intelligent data analysis and automation.

Objectives

  • To develop an AI-driven platform for real-time city data analysis
  • To address urban challenges such as traffic congestion, energy efficiency, and waste management
  • To implement machine learning algorithms for predictive maintenance of city infrastructure
  • To improve overall quality of life for citizens through smart technology integration

Expected Outcomes

  • A functional prototype of the AI-powered Smart City Management System
  • Comprehensive documentation of the system architecture and AI models used
  • Analysis of the system's effectiveness in improving city operations
  • A roadmap for future enhancements and potential citywide implementation

Methodology

  1. Extensive research on existing smart city technologies and AI applications
  2. Design of system architecture and selection of appropriate AI/ML models
  3. Development of the core platform and integration with IoT devices
  4. Implementation of data analytics and visualization components
  5. Rigorous testing in simulated urban environments
  6. Performance evaluation and optimization

Timeline

  • Project Proposal Submission: September 15, 2024
  • Design Document Completion: October 30, 2024
  • Prototype Development: January 15, 2025
  • Testing Phase: February 28, 2025
  • Final Report Submission: April 15, 2025
  • Project Presentation: May 1, 2025

Resources Required

  • Hardware: High-performance computing cluster, IoT sensors and devices
  • Software: TensorFlow, PyTorch, Apache Kafka, Elasticsearch
  • Data: Urban datasets from local government agencies, real-time IoT data streams
  • Expertise: Machine Learning, Big Data Analytics, IoT Integration, Urban Planning
